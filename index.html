ECMAScript 6: Overview

ECMAScript 6 is the 2015 instalment of the world's most popular programming language commonly known as JavaScript. ES6 brought in many new features, some having a profound impact on how the language is used. Constants, Arrow functions and classes added a new level of formality to a very loose language whilst promises simplified JavaScripts somewhat messy asynchronicity. Let this be your guide to all that is new in ES6.
